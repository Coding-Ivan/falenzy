About me

Music Producer || Artist

‘Falenzy Usher’ is a leading Gospel Singer and Music Producer, Songwriter and Instrumentalist from Kenya- East-Africa. He plays both the guitar and piano. Born in the Kisii County ,Kenya, Falenzy is from a humble background, having spent some part of his childhood as a humble kid then later got through his primary and secondary education and became a Super Gospel Singer.
